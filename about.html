About Us

Reliable and express logistics and transport solutions That do not waste your time!

We take this opportunity to introduce ourselves and provide you with the following information about our Company and what we can do for you. We have grown steadily to become one of the leading transport service providers to Guinness Ghana Breweries Limited (GGBL). Our goal is to understand your business and provide a customised transport solution that includes; competitive rates, an authentic approach to real customer service and a proactive approach to meet your expectations. The business has been built on service and attention to detail employing only the best available Sub-Contractors and Company drivers & staff. Currently we are providing customized transport service to Guinness Ghana Breweries Limited and Nestle Ghana Limited.

With an extensive fleet of over 51 vehicles ranging in size from five (5) tonne container vans up to extendable semi-trailers and 40ft trailers. Our Subcontractors are checked regularly to ensure they are maintaining their vehicles and equipment. We insist on them carrying out all the necessary routine maintenance and repair works at regular basis to ensure the vehicles are roadworthy all time round. Our drivers have all the necessary training, equipment and PPE to ensure a quality image when representing your company. Supplying such a diverse range of specialized transport service to GGBL and Nestle, who both represent the largest beverage companies in the country and a wealth of industry knowledge at our disposal, we are confident we can offer your company a complete transport business solution.
